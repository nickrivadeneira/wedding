Directions

Train Directions from NYC

Take the Metro North (Hudson Line) from Grand Central Station to Greystone. The trains run once an hour and take 35 minutes. If there are no cabs available at the station, please call Yonkers Car Services at 914-423-3111.


Driving Directions to Alder Manor

From South, NYC & Brooklyn - Take the West Side Highway North. Stay on the highway as it turns to Henry Hudson Parkway and then to Saw Mill Parkway. Take Saw Mill Parkway to the Executive Boulevard exit (exit 9) in Yonkers. It is a right hand exit. After 1 mile, Executive Boulevard dead-ends into North Broadway. Turn left. After 0.2 miles, Alder Manor will be on your right. (sign says "Foxfire School")

From the North - Take the Saw Mill Parkway South to Executive Boulevard in Yonkers. After 1 mile, Executive Boulevard dead-ends into North Broadway. Turn left. After 0.2 miles, Alder Manor will be on your right. (sign says "Foxfire School")

From Connecticut & the East - Take I-684 South to Exit 5, Saw Mill Parkway South. Take the Saw Mill Parkway South 25 miles to Executive Boulevard in Yonkers. After 1 mile, Executive Boulevard dead-ends into North Broadway. Turn left. After 0.2 miles, Alder Manor will be on your right. (sign says "Foxfire School")
