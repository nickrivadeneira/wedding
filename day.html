Both the ceremony and reception will be held at historic Alder Manor in Yonkers, NY. Directions

Train Directions from NYC

Take the Metro North (Hudson Line) from Grand Central Station to Greystone. The trains run once an hour and take 35 minutes. If there are no cabs available at the station, please call Yonkers Car Services at 914-423-3111.


Driving Directions to Alder Manor

From NYC - Take the West Side Highway North. Stay on the highway as it turns to Henry Hudson Parkway and then to the Saw Mill Parkway North. Take Saw Mill Parkway 4.4 miles to exit 9, Executive Boulevard, in Yonkers. After 1 mile, Executive Boulevard dead-ends into North Broadway. Turn left onto North Broadway. After 0.2 miles, Alder Manor will be on your right (sign says "Foxfire School"). The venue will be on your right hand side after you pass the sign.

From Rockland County & the North - Take I-287 E over the Tappan Zee Bridge. Just after the bridge, take the exit to I-87 S toward NYC. After 1 mile, take exit 7A, Saw Mill Parkway South. Take the Saw Mill Parkway South 5.8 miles and make a right onto Executive Boulevard in Yonkers. After 1 mile, Executive Boulevard dead-ends into North Broadway. Turn left onto North Broadway. After 0.2 miles, Alder Manor will be on your right (sign says "Foxfire School"). The venue will be on your right hand side after you pass the sign.

Take the Saw Mill Parkway South 5.8 miles and make a right onto Executive Boulevard in Yonkers. After 1 mile, Executive Boulevard dead-ends into North Broadway. Turn left onto North Broadway. After 0.2 miles, Alder Manor will be on your right (sign says "Foxfire School"). The venue will be on your right hand side after you pass the sign.
